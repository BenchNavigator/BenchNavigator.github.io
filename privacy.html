Privacy notice

Last updated: 29 June 2026

BenchNavigator is a static, client-side website with no backend, no accounts, and no database. It serves only public benchmark metadata derived from Hugging Face and arXiv. The only data it collects about visitors is described below.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how the site is used (for example, how many people visit and which pages and features are popular). It is configured in a privacy-conscious way:

  • IP addresses are anonymized (anonymize_ip).
  • Google Signals and advertising personalization are disabled.
  • No advertising or cross-site tracking is performed.

Google Analytics sets cookies and sends usage data (such as page views, approximate region, device type, and in-app events like searches and outbound link clicks) to Google, which processes it on our behalf. See Google’s Privacy Policy.

What we do not collect

  • No names, emails, or account information — there are no logins.
  • No advertising profiles and no selling of data.
  • No data beyond what the analytics described above collect.

How to opt out

  • Enable “Do Not Track” or tracking protection in your browser.
  • Use a content/ad blocker — analytics simply won’t load, and the site still works.
  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Contact

Questions or requests? Open an issue on the project repository.
